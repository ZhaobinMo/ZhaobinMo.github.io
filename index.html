Zhaobin Mo - 莫钊镔

I am currently a fourth-year Ph. D. student in Civil Engineering at Columbia University. I will be graduating in Dec. 2023 under the advision of Prof. Xuan Di at (Ditect Lab). I received my Bachelor of Engineering from the School of Vehicle and Mobility. I was awarded president fellow and Mindlin scholar in Columbia University.

My research focused on understanding the theory and design of AI models in transporation that leverge physics prior knowledge encoded in the data-generating mechanism. Broadly speaking, my research belongs to scientific machine learning, which concerns regularizing and explaining black-box AI models with physics prior knowldge. I have also worked on various topics in data science, including reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, and deep generative models. My email is zm2303 at columbia dot edu.
